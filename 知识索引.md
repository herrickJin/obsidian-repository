## 🔥 最近学习

```dataview
TABLE 
  file.folder as 分类,
  difficulty as 难度,
  status as 状态
FROM "03-Resources"
WHERE file.name != "README" AND status = "🌱 学习中"
SORT file.mtime DESC
LIMIT 10
```
